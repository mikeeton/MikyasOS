# Business Intelligence Decision Platform

Status: Internal Product Design Standard

## Purpose

Business intelligence in mikyasOS exists to improve decisions. Charts, reports, KPIs, and
forecasts are useful only when they help a user understand what changed, why it matters,
who owns the next step, and what should happen next.

## Decision Flow

Analytics should move through this sequence:

Data -> information -> insight -> recommendation -> decision -> automation -> business
improvement.

Dashboards must avoid dead ends. A revenue metric should drill into invoices, customers,
projects, meetings, documents, AI analysis, and recommended actions.

## Executive Command Centre

The executive view should answer:

- How is the business?
- What changed today?
- What requires attention?
- What opportunities exist?
- What risks exist?
- What should happen next?

This makes the dashboard a decision surface rather than a reporting archive.

## KPI Standard

Every KPI should support:

- Current value
- Previous value
- Percentage change
- Trend
- Forecast
- Target
- Variance
- Explanation
- Drill-down
- History
- Comparison
- Owner
- Confidence

## Business Health Score

Every organisation should have one composite health signal with explainable components:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Cash flow
- Customer health
- Project delivery
- Employee capacity
- Automation success
- Operational risk
- Security
- AI confidence
- Growth

The score should always link to the underlying records.

## AI Insights

AI analytics must explain numbers with sources. A useful insight should include:

- What changed
- Primary reason
- Secondary reason
- Impact
- Confidence
- Related records
- Recommended action
- Automation opportunity

AI should never hide uncertainty.

## Reports And Explorer

Reports should support daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly, and custom periods, with
saved filters, scheduled delivery, printable output, export formats, and version history.

The data explorer should let non-technical users ask business questions, filter records,
visualise results, and export outcomes without writing SQL.

## Golden Principle

Analytics is not a mirror. It is a steering system. Every analytics surface should help
the user choose a better next move.
